Toongabbie

Location Details

Toongabbie is located in the Blacktown/Parramatta area of Sydney's Western suburbs.

There are 417 SDA dwellings in the two areas today, with 142 in the Blacktown and 275 in the Parramatta areas.

Both areas are served well by accessible public transport and hospitals, as well as several UWS campuses and Public Schools for Specifc Purposes.

While the areas are close together, they differ in key ways; the type of existing housing stock, the languages spoken, and the cultural backgrounds of residents.

There are 5342 people working in Residential Care across Parramatta or Blacktown, with 3312 staying within the two areas for work.

Language ability in the areas 11

Compared to Greater Sydney, more people speak a second language. When communicating with communities here, it's important to note that over 10% of those in Parramatta do not speak English well or at all.
