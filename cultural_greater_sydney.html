Green is below average and orange is above average. The intensity of the colour indicates the strength of the difference. The table shows the top SA4s across Greater Sydney

Data sources

All data is sourced from the Australian Bureau of Statistics, 2016 Census, Census Table Builder https://www.abs.gov.au/websitedbs/D3310114.nsf/Home/2016%20TableBuilder

Please note that all references to Greater Sydney excludes the Central Coast

Ancestry is a multiple response question in the census. Proportions have been calculated by adding up first and second ancestry and then diving by total population of the SA2.
